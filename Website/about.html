About Us

American Shed Builders Inc. has been designing and building storage sheds since 1991 for over  20,000 satisfied customers. Our friendly staff of high-quality craftsmen have more than  75 years of combined manufacturing experience. At American Shed Builders Inc., we construct all of our sheds from top to bottom, inside and out, with only the best quality materials and construction! (No economy grade, shop grade, mill grade, or # 3-grade materials are used!)

We build all sheds on your site, no need to remove fences, bushes, lawn sprinkler systems, etc.

Reasons Why You Should Choose American Shed Builders:

  • Single-day construction.*
  • Buy direct from the manufacturer.
  • Compare our construction to the competition.
  • Built directly on your yard! No trucks on your lawn, no removal of bushes or fences necessary.
  • Our honest, knowledgeable, and friendly staff is here to answer all of your questions.
  • No subcontractors.
  • No one beats our "bumper-to-bumper" warranty.
  • Custom color matching to your home's painted or vinyl exterior.
  • Our reputation is second to none for quality construction at affordable prices.

*Some sheds may take more than a single-day due to size, customization, or inclement weather.
